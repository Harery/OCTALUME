## Executive Summary

This business case proposes the development of an enterprise-grade e-commerce platform to replace our legacy online store. The current system is experiencing frequent downtime, poor mobile experience, and inability to handle peak traffic during sales events. The proposed platform will address these issues while providing a foundation for future growth.

**Key Highlights**:
- **Investment**: $2.5M over 18 months
- **Expected ROI**: 180% over 3 years
- **Break-even**: Month 14
- **Payback Period**: 18 months

**Recommendation**: **GO** - Proceed with development

---

## 1. Business Problem

### 1.1 Current State
Our current e-commerce platform was built in 2015 using outdated technology. The system experiences:
- Average downtime of 8 hours per month
- 40% cart abandonment rate
- Poor mobile experience (60% mobile traffic bounce rate)
- Inability to scale during peak events (Black Friday, etc.)

### 1.2 Problem Statement
The legacy platform is limiting our growth potential and negatively impacting customer experience. Competitors with modern platforms are capturing market share.

### 1.3 Impact
If left unaddressed:
- Revenue loss: $5M annually (conservative estimate)
- Market share decline: 15% year-over-year
- Customer churn: Increasing by 5% per quarter
- Brand reputation: Declining due to poor performance

---

## 2. Proposed Solution

### 2.1 Solution Overview
Build a modern, cloud-native e-commerce platform with:
- Microservices architecture for scalability
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Advanced personalization and recommendations
- Omnichannel capabilities
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance

### 2.2 Key Features
- User accounts with social login
- Advanced product catalog with faceted search
- Shopping cart with saved items
- Multiple payment options
- Order management and tracking
- Admin dashboard with analytics
- Marketing and promotion tools

### 2.3 Target Users
- **Primary**: Online shoppers aged 25-45
- **Secondary**: Enterprise customers (B2B)
- **Tertiary**: International customers
